Super Tennis

Super Nintendo


Released 1991

Reviewed by Thomas, 12/07/2015

The game is, well you should know because of the title, Tennis. It feels like your playing at Wimbledon because of how it is set up. There are 4 different hits: Long ball (high ball), Slice (straight ball), Power ball (quick ball) and Normal.

There are lots of different players you can pick from who all look different. There are lady players and men players.

You can pick from 3 different courts: Hard (really bouncy), Lawn (Normal amount of bounce) and Clay (not bouncy at all).

I like the graphics in the game because they tried to make it the most realistic as possible. The sound is really good to, and it sounds like you are really hitting the ball. I like the music when you change ends.

Playing Super Tennis is great fun especially defeating parents, ha! Serving is hard though and I kept getting double faults.

The Verdict

You should play this game because it is great fun if you play with bunch of friends and do a knockout tournament.
