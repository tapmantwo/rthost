California Games

Sega Master System


Released 1989

Reviewed by Thomas, 05/04/2015

In this game, you play in a Californian Games competition. This has 6 events, which are Half Pipe, Foot Bag, Surfing, Skating, BMX and Flying Disc.

The events are extremely cool, and you have to concentrate to play them well. The surfing event makes me concentrate the most. Some events are quite hard to learn, and some are easy to learn.

My favourite event is Foot bag, where you kick a ball in the air and these are the tricks you can do: Five in a row, Half axle, Full axle, Axle Foley, Horseshoe, Double Arch, Doda, Reverse Doda, Jester, Head Banger, Hit the gull

My favourite move is Horseshoe (where you flick the ball up with one foot and then again with the other). The Axle Foley (when you flick the ball up in the air, turn 540 degrees and hit the ball again) is quite hard to do but I have done it :)

The graphics are excellent and I like the backgrounds. I dont like the music because it plays over and over again and gets repetitive.

My sister thinks the half pipe is too hard, and foot bag takes a lot of time to learn.

This game can get quite hard but the more you play the easier it gets.

The Verdict

I recommend you play this game as it is jolly good fun.
