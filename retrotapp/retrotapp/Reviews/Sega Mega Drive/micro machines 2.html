Micro Machines 2 - Turbo Tournament 2

Sega Mega Drive


Released 1994

Reviewed by Thomas, 24/08/2015

The game is all about racing toy machines around places like the bath, the kitchen, a beach, a pool table and a lot more tracks.

In total there are 27 tracks and they are all amazing and well made!

You can also pick from different people and if you don’t like their name you can change it.

Playing micro machines is great fun even though it is hard it is enjoyable. I like how you don’t just play in cars - you play in boats, helicopters and bikes for different tracks.

I like how they made the tracks a mixture of difficulties. Some are easy and some are hard and my hardest one so far is Driller Killer.

You can play single player or multiple players and personally I like single player more because you get more of a challenge as you are racing the computer.

The Verdict

I think you should play this game because it’s very well made and it is jolly good fun!
