Magicland Dizzy

C64, Cassette

Released 1990

Reviewed by Thomas, 26/07/2015

You are an egg with legs and hands, called Dizzy. Your friends have been imprisoned with evil spells by an evil wizard called Zaks, you have a task to save all your friends.

There are puzzles for you to solve to save your friends e.g. when you go to get your first friend who’s been turned into a frog you have to collect her so the Prince can kiss her to break the spell. Some puzzles are hard and some are easy to work out.

I like the music because it’s bouncy and it is a good match with the game. I also like the graphics and working out the different puzzles.

Sometimes I wish you could teleport to other places. E.g. once you get Daisy on one side of the map you have to go to the other side of the map! The game is fun but the controls can be frustrating at times, whenever I try to walk I jump. I also don’t like ONLY three lives because when you get really far and lose all your lives there are no continues (I know annoying!).

The Verdict

You should play this game if you like platform and puzzle games, but don’t play much if you get frustrated easily.
