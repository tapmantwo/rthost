Frogger

Atari 2600


Released 1982

Reviewed by Thomas, 03/05/2015

You are a frog and you have to cross a road whilst dodging cars, and crossing a river (avoiding crocodiles and snakes) to get safely home to the river bank. Then you do this four more times to pass the level. (It’s not as easy as it sounds!).

Playing can be hard when you first start but the more you practise the easier it gets. You can easily get stressed out if you can’t get home in the far left corner, I definitely do!

TIP: DON’T go on the blue dots in the river because they disintegrate and you lose a life. :)

I like the music because it’s kind of catchy. I like the graphics because they tried their best to make the cars look like cars and the frog look like a frog. I also like the animation.

The Verdict

The game is very good to play, it is all about timing and that’s a good skill for games.
